About Us

Management Solutions Inc. has been providing professional support services to non-profit associations and societies across the state since 1994. Dedication to our clients, attention to detail, client satisfaction and always delivering on schedule has served us well in maintaining our long term relationships with the clients we serve. We believe that our success is based on the success of our clients.

The Team





Candace Cansler

Founder and CEO


Candace Cansler, the founder and CEO of Management Solutions has twenty-five plus years of experience in association management. Candace founded Management Solutions when a past employer asked her to manage a national start up association in 1992. Candace enthusiastically took on the challenge and all of these years later she is still managing that same start up alongside an array of other clients.


Since that first client, Candace has enjoyed serving as Executive Director to many state and national organizations. Candace has planned and executed hundreds of events and has managed all aspects of event planning for association clients. Management Solutions has been successful by providing the highest level of client services and delivering what we promise.

Our success is based on the success of our clients.
